Salvadorean Pupusas

The most delicius dish across Latam, great for breakfast, lunch and dinner, no Salvadorean reunion could be complete without this flavorfull dish

This is a fairly authentic version of the popular Salvadorean dish. It does take some time, however it is well worth the effort!
You can purchase pre-made chicharron at specialty markets, however this recipe tastes just like it.
I also make the salsa roja and curtido ahead of time (the salsa freezes very well, and the curtido can stay in the fridge at least a week).

Ingredients

Salsa Roja:

  • 4 cups plain tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 bunches fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed (Optional)
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • salt to taste
